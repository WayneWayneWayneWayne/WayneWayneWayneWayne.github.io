APT 29 hacks Mircosoft

Last week, A Russian backed hacker group with the official name of APT 29, better known as “cozy bear” hacked into the Microsoft Executives emails. This was the same group that hacked the Solar Winds supply chain back in 2020, allowing them access to the Pentagon, and the DNC in 2015.
